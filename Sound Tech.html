Sound Tech

Provide set up of microphones, monitors, etc., as required by the music minister, pastor, worship director or staff. Sound techs monitor sound, activate microphones and play appropriate music and CD's. Sound Techs may be asked to run sound/music for special events, such as weddings and funerals
