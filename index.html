STATEMENT OF FAITH

What We Believe

United Methodists affirm the faith shared by all Christians, with particular emphasis on God's grace and on Christian living.

The mission of The United Methodist Church is to make disciples of Jesus Christ for the transformation of the world.

Who We Are

The people of Brooke Road Church are a warm, friendly community of faith, located in southeastern Rockford, Illinois. We give thanks for the uniqueness of each person and we celebrate the diversity of Gods Family. We welcome all people regardless of race, religious denomination, or sexuality. As we journey together, we strive to walk together in community, as we strengthen our personal walk with Christ in a manner that is nonthreatening.

How We Live

Everyones experiences of faith and walking this journey are on different levels and by no means is one persons faith/walk better than another.
Children and youth are extremely important to us at Brooke Road Church. We want our children to feel love, care and safety as they grow in relationship with God and others. Brooke Road Church believes that mission and outreach in the name of Christ are essential for expressing our faith.
As we serve, we broaden the journey to include others in the grace, peace, and fellowship together, we journey together believing that lives can be enriched transformed by love and forgiveness. We invite you to join our community any time as we journey in faith, hope, and joy
